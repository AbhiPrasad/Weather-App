## Weather App

Tells you the weather at your location

---

Using:

* HTML5 Geolocation
* [Google Maps API](https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/geocoding/start?csw=1#ReverseGeocoding)
* [Open Weather API](http://openweathermap.org/)
